Advantages of On-Premise Licensing

  • Predictable cost: Fixed license fee, no surprise price increases.
  • Scalable: Costs don't multiply as user count grows.
  • Independence: Software continues working after contract ends, data stays with you.
  • Long-term savings: On-premise model is typically more economical for 3+ years of use.
  • Budget planning: Can be planned as a fixed line item in your annual IT budget.

Choosing the Right Model

SaaS isn't bad for every organization â€” it can make sense for small teams and short-term projects. However, on-premise should be preferred when:

  • 100+ users are planned
  • Usage beyond 3 years is expected
  • Sensitive data is processed (healthcare, finance, government)
  • Regulatory compliance requirements exist
  • Cost predictability is important
